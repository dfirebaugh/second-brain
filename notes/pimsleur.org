* Pimsleur
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: pimsleur
:END:
[[https://www.pimsleur.com/][Pimsleur]] is an app that has excellent
mobile app that allows you to listen to dialogues and participate in
[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speech_shadowing][shadowing]]. This
seems to be an excellent way to learn some initial every-day-use phrases
in a language. However, it doesn't have a monthly fee.
