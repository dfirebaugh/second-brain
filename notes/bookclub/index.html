Book Club

I'm in a bookclub with some friends. We take turns picking books. At any point we can (as a group) decide to shelf a book (which basically means we might come back to it, but we probably wont).

We typically read about 150 pages every two weeks.

Podcast

we are testing out recording the book club meetings as a podcast.

Rating system

I'll loosely slap a rating on these based on how much I like the book. 5 means i liked it a lot.

  • 🕮 - one point
  • 🗏 - half a point

(e.g. 🕮🕮🗏 == 2.5)
